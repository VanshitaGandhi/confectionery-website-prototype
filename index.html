ABOUT US

Our company provides you with the best to satisfy your sweet cravings and enrich your overall experience. We have established 57 stores all over Ottawa, Canada in the span of past 3 years.The new, bigger space allowed the store to grow, develop and flourish.
Our growth is attributed both to our customers desire for satisfying their sugar tooth, as well as our commitment to providing you with the best quality food.
Coco's Chocolatiers has consistantly won the "People's Choice Candy Store" award from NEXT Magazine since the award began in 2016 ,as well as FOREVER Magazine's "Best Quality Candy Store" award since it started the category in 2000.We receive regular press and media coverage.We serve approximately 55 different flavours and lines of candy, cakes, muffins, donuts, pastries, danishes, swiss rolls and chocolates.Our transporters travel the world so we may provide our customers with the ultimate sugar experience. Our products are sourced from Germany, Spain, Italy, Switzerland, Great Britain and China.
Coco Chocolatiers' stores smell of fresh and clean candy with a pop of colour. the stores' personality cinsists of kindness and friendliness.
